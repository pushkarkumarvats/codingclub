About the Club


The Coding Club - Online Degree Program (ODP), IIT Guwahati, is a vibrant, student-driven community officially launched on March 5, 2025. Born out of the enthusiastic spirit of students from the BSc (Hons.) in Data Science & Artificial Intelligence, the club serves as a hub for developers, designers, and tech enthusiasts across the online degree cohort.


We are committed to fostering innovation, collaboration, and continuous learning. Whether you're a complete beginner exploring your first lines of code or an experienced developer building advanced projects, our club offers a welcoming environment where you can:


  • Learn and explore the latest technologies
  • Collaborate on real-world projects
  • Engage in coding challenges and hackathons
  • Access mentorship and peer support
  • Network with like-minded individuals across the country

At the core of our mission is the desire to bridge the gap between distance and connection, by building a strong, interactive, and growth-oriented community among online learners.
